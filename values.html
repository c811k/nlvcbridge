VISION & MISSION VALUES

VISION


"A PLACE TO CONNECT." (WITH GOD AND WITH OTHERS)

THEME VERSE


“And let us consider how to stir up one another to love and good works, not neglecting to meet together, as is the habit of some, but encouraging one another, and all the more as you see the Day drawing near.”

Hebrews 10:24-25

MISSION


1. To exalt God with our lives for His glory - 1 Corinthians 10:31

2. To edify the brethren to love and good deeds - Hebrews 10:24-25

3. To equip the saints for the work of the ministry - Ephesians 4:11-12

4. To evangelize the world with the Gospel of the Lord Jesus Christ - Matthew 28:16-20

5. To exemplify Christ-likeness to a watching world - John 13:34-35
